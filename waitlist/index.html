The Waitlist Process

Your guide to the first come, first served waitlist at Eastern Arizona College

Your Journey

From joining the list to claiming a seat, here is the automated path a student follows.

1. You Join the Waitlist

When you try to register for a full class, choose the "Waitlist" option. You'll get an email confirming you're on the list.

↓
Pro Tip

Check your student email every day! Your 24-hour time limit to register starts the moment we send the offer.
